Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy was last modified on Fri Nov 24 2023 22:35:33 GMT+0800 (Malaysia Time).

Mugen Project Studio (henceforth referred to as "the Company") has implemented the following privacy policy (henceforth referred to as "this privacy policy") concerning the handling of personal information data about living individuals that either (1) contains a personal identification code (henceforth referred to as “personal information”) or (2) identifies a specific individual by name, date of birth, or other description stored in the data (including data that can be easily cross-referenced with other data to identify a specific individual). Terms are defined in this privacy policy in accordance with the requirements of the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (hereinafter referred to as the "Personal Information Protection Act"), unless otherwise indicated in this policy.

We understand the significance of handling personal information appropriately and have established a personal information protection policy based on the belief that the Company is in a position to show an example for the proper handling of personal information as an entertainment company that contributes to society. Our employees will cooperate to adhere to this and will take all reasonable steps to safeguard individuals' rights and interests as well as personal information.



  • Whenever we collect personal data, we will make every effort to clearly state the reason for usage and only collect data that is required to fulfill that goal.
  • We shall only use personal data for the purposes for which the individual has granted permission. Furthermore, we shall devise and execute protocols to undertake essential actions to avert inadvertent use.
  • We shall handle the personal data we collect responsibly and never give it away or reveal it to a third party without the individual's permission.
  • If we receive personal information in writing directly from an individual, we will only collect such information to the degree required after providing the individual with notice of our company's name, the contact details of the personal information protection manager, the intended use, etc.
  • When we outsource the processing of personal information to a third party, we require the company by contract not to leak or give the information to a third party and will oversee the company accordingly.
  • Upon receiving such queries through our contact, we will respond to complaints and consultations about the personal information we have.
  • We shall handle the personal information we keep in an accurate and up-to-date way within the scope required for the purpose of use, and we will take reasonable safety measures against the risk of leakage, loss, or damage to personal information in order to avoid and repair such occurrences.
  • We shall follow all laws and regulations, government guidelines, and other norms governing the processing of personal information.

Perpetration of Personal Information

Any information that may be used to identify a specific user is what we refer to as personal information, and this includes, but is not limited to: ​  Information that users submit to us through forms they fill out to become members of our services, buy forms, and other relevant forms.  Other information that the user has supplied about our services. Unless an external collection company and we have reached a confidentiality agreement allowing the latter to collect this information, we do not disclose any personal information to other parties. In these situations, prior to the collection of information, the user is made aware of any such agreement. Moreover, we have no obligation or liability for the information disclosed by external websites, as this Privacy Policy does not apply to websites that only link to our services. Please review the privacy policies of each website in question for information on these situations. Furthermore, no personally identifiable information is revealed by the data our services give. ​

​Purpose of collection of Information

We will use fair and lawful methods to collect personal information, and we'll only use it for the purposes listed below. Please be aware that if there is a purpose for using specific personal information that is not covered by the following purposes, we will either use the data after notifying the person about the purpose of use when it is obtained through other means or after getting the person's consent when it is obtained directly in written form.

  • Personal information of applicants for employment
    • To contact applicants
    • To verify nationality
  • Personal information of participants in auditions and competitions.
    • To verify nationality
    • To report the result
  • To make sure proper hire or partner contracts are signed and followed Malaysia law
    • To ship the prize
  • Personal information about business partners
    • To access business partners
    • To signed contracts
  • Personal information of individual when holding events.
    • To access people about the events
    • To operate the event

Disclosure to third parties of personal  information

If the information collected from the customer falls under the category of personal information, the Company must not disclose the personal information to a third party without the customer's prior consent. This does not apply in the following circumstances. When a court, public prosecutor's office, police, tax office, or other organisation with equivalent power requests the disclosure of personal information. When a firm that does business on our behalf in order to provide products and services to users need information (for example, a delivery address for a courier service). In certain circumstances, we and the external entity have a data confidentiality agreement, and the latter is not allowed to use information beyond what is required for the relevant business. Furthermore, if a partner company collects and utilises user information through us via an event or similar, the user will be notified of the arrangement, and we will only disclose the information to the partner company with the user's consent.​

​Changes to privacy policy

The Company has the right to modify the terms of this privacy statement. If any modification to the contents of this privacy policy involves a change that must be communicated to or agreed upon by the customer under relevant laws and ordinances, the Company will follow the procedure in accordance with the applicable law or regulation.

​Access Logs

When users browse our service's web page, we capture the information in the form of an access log on our server and retain it for a certain amount of time. The date and time of access, IP address, browser type, and cookie information are all kept in the access log. These are utilised as server operating information to improve server accessibility, server administration, and to investigate unauthorised usage. ​ Inquiries Regarding Registration Data The user can amend the personal information entered at the time of membership registration, etc., at any time on the prescribed page in our services. If you are unable to make changes yourself for any reason, please contact us.
